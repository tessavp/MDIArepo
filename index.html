Fireweed Podcast

Fireweed, BCIT’s first branded podcast has launched

In British Columbia, fireweed is the very first plant to grow back after a forest fire. Humble and tenacious, bright and prolific, fireweed is a symbol of hope, resilience, and adaptability – things the world could all use a little more of right now. Fireweed, consists of six 20-minute episodes, which celebrates stories of people who have persevered and re-written their story in the face of disruption.

During an unparalleled period of profound adjustment in the way we work, learn, and socialize, the stories of Fireweed will provide a unique educational experience for listeners that features guests on topics including accessibility, sustainability, cybersecurity, shifting careers, advancing education, and the future of transportation.

Follow Fireweed and listen wherever you get your podcast
